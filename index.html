Ukrainian borscht is a hearty soup made from beef and various vegetables, dominated by root vegetables and cabbage, and the characteristic dark red color of the soup is the color of beets. Our borscht is not only vegetable and beetroot soup, we cook it with passion. In our food truck you will find dishes for every taste just look at the Menu
